Nuclear Gradients of MCSCF

  • Well established gradient theory for CASSCF, we use the same gradient expressions for HCISCF
  • But there's a catch: the expressions aren't exact for HCISCF
  • Our calculations have two sources of error:
    1. HCI approximates FCI
    2. Gradient expressions aren't exact
  • We can control error of the energy and gradient with \(\epsilon_1\)
  • If the wave functions are close enough to exact, the error from these approximations is zero
