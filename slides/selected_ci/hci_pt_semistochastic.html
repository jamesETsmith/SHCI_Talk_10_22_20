Reducing Stochastic Noise

$$ E_2 = \sum_{D_{a} \in \mathcal{C}} \frac{1}{E_{a}-E_{0}} \left( \sum_{D_{i} \in \mathcal{V}_{stoc. }} ^{(\epsilon_2)} H_{a i} c_{i} \right)^2 $$

We can reduce the stochastic noise by calculating as much of \( E_2 \) deterministically as possible

$$ E_2 = E_2^D (\epsilon_2^D) + (E_2^S (\epsilon_2) - E_2^S (\epsilon_2^D)) $$
