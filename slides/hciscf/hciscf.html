MCSCF with HCI

To optimize orbitals, we rely on the Hellmann-Feynam Thm.:

$$ \frac{\partial E}{\partial \kappa} = \bra{\Psi_0} \frac{\partial H}{\partial \kappa} \ket{\Psi_0} $$ Enforce variationality of \( E_{HCI} = E_0 + E_2 \) $$ \frac{\partial E_{HCI}}{\partial \kappa} = \bra{\Psi_0} \frac{\partial H}{\partial \kappa} \ket{\Psi_0} + ... (\text{lagrangian terms}) $$
