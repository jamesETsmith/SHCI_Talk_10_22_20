Relaxed Singlet-Triplet Gaps

Goals

  • Optimize the structures of the singlet and triplet states
  • Do the gaps converge with \( \epsilon_1 \)?

James E. T. Smith, JL, SS (2020) In prep.

  • CAS(28e,28o): full valence space
  • Two step procedure:
    1. Find equilibrium structures for singlet/triplet, calculate gaps (in blue)
    2. Run "tight" single-shot HCI, calculate gaps (in orange)
  • \( \epsilon_1 \) = 10-2 ~103 dets
  • \( \epsilon_1 \) = 10-3 ~104 dets
  • \( \epsilon_1 \) = 10-4 ~106 dets
  • \( \epsilon_1 \) = 5x10-5 ~3x106 dets
